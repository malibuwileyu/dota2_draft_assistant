    Bio[edit]

    I bring my lightning whip not to punish souls, but only to hasten them toward the inevitable exit.
    ▶️  Razor, the Lightning Revenant
    Lore:
    Among the emblematic powers that populate the Underscape, Razor the Lightning Revenant is one of the most feared. With his whip of lightning, he patrols the Narrow Maze, that treacherous webwork of passages by which the souls of the dead are sorted according to their own innate intelligence, cunning and persistence. Drifting above the Maze, Razor looks down on the baffled souls below, and delivers jolts of scalding electricity that both punish and quicken the souls as they decide their own fates, hurrying on toward luminous exits or endlessly dark pits. Razor is the eternal embodiment of a dominating power, abstract and almost clinical in his application of power. Yet he has a lordly air that suggests he takes a sardonic satisfaction in his work.
    Voice:
    Eric Newsome (Responses)

    Downtime: 0.5
    • The Forked Lightning ability effects are applied as follows:
        1. Applies its magical spell damage instance before the enemy's ability cast on Razor.
          • Deals spell damage before the ability is cast, if the enemy unit dies to the damage instance, the ability is unused.
          • Certain sources that usually prevents damage do not save the enemy caster if the damage instance is lethal. This includes the following sources:
            • Damage negation sources.
            • Spell immunity sources.
        2. The original ability cast.
    • Storm Surge uses pseudo-random distribution, and has two proc conditions:
        • Has a chance to proc Forked Lightning upon a successful attack on Razor, with the ability effects applied as follows:
          1. Applies the magical spell damage to the attack source, this component only affects enemy units.
          2. Applies the debuff to the attack source.
          3. Search for valid enemy units for the Forked Lightning component.
          • Has a Global proc radius and an ensure proc chance on every offensive unit-targeted ability cast on Razor, including certain unit and ground-targeted abilities.
            • Procs the Forked Lightning component upon the offensive projectile launch.
            • The offensive ability cast still takes effect even if the ability source dies to the spell damage applied.
          • Affects the following units:
            • Fogged units.
            • Invisible units.
    • Does not proc from the following attack sources:
      • Buildings
      • Does not apply to, and considers the primary target not a valid enemy unit when affected by the following sources:
        • Debuff immune sources.
        • Hidden sources.
        • Invulnerable sources.
      • On secondary targets:
        • The search radius is centered around the primary enemy unit that procs the Forked Lightning component.
          • If the primary target is not a valid enemy unit, Forked Lightning searches for 3 secondary targets upon proc based on the primary enemy unit.
        • Has no secondary target priorities.
        • Does not affect the following secondary targets when procced via an offensive attack:
          • Fogged units.
          • Invisible units.
          • Untargetable units.
      • The spell damage is considered reflection damage, which prevents it from benefitting from spell lifesteal.
    • FACET NOTES
      The Thunderhead   facet now procs Forked Lightning within the Eye of the Storm radius.
      • An enemy unit may only be hit by one Thunderhead proc Forked Lightning strike at a time per proc.
        • A lone target is not hit multiple times per active Eye of the Storm strike instance.
    • ILLUSIONS
      Razor's illusions fully benefit from the bonus movement speed component.
      • However, the illusions does not release Forked Lightning upon proc.

    Trivia[edit]

    • On the Perfect World client, Razor's name was literally translated into "Shaving Blade" - 剃刀 (tìdāo).
      • It was not until November 18, 2022 Patch that he was renamed to similar-sounding Chinese loanwords - 雷泽 (léizé).
    • His attack response ▶️ "Ride the Lightning!" is also the name of a Metallica's album and song (which references execution via electric chair).
    • Razor's response upon picking the Aegis of the Immortal ▶️ "There is a lightning that never goes out." is a reference to a song "There is a light that never goes out" by The Smiths.
    • ▶️ "Conduct me where you will.' is also a line said by Scrooge in "A Christmas Carol".
    • Warcraft 3 similarities:
      • Razor uses the model of the Lightning Revenant, a neutral melee creep.
      • Razor's title is also directly taken from that unit.
      • Razor's old ability, Chain Lightning, was identical to the Far Seer's Chain Lightning.
      • Razor's old ability, Forked Lightning, was identical to the Naga Sea Witch's Forked Lightning.
      • Razor's old abilities, Fury and Frenzy, were to the Troll Berserker's Berserk.
      • Razor has a 1/8 chance to spawn in with the name "Gillette", a reference to the Gillette, known for their shaving razors.
