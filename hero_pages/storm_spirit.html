    Bio[edit]

    Everyone complains about the weather…well, I'm doing something about it!
    ▶️  Raijin Thunderkeg, the Storm Spirit
    Aliases:
    SS, Raijin, Thunderkeg
    Lore:
    Storm Spirit is literally a force of nature--the wild power of wind and weather, bottled in human form. And a boisterous, jovial, irrepressible form it is! As jolly as a favorite uncle, he injects every scene with crackling energy. But it was not always thus, and there was tragedy in his creation. Generations ago, in the plains beyond the Wailing Mountains, a good people lay starving in drought and famine. A simple elementalist, Thunderkeg by name, used a forbidden spell to summon the spirit of the storm, asking for rain. Enraged at this mortal’s presumption, the Storm Celestial known as Raijin lay waste to the land, scouring it bare with winds and flood. Thunderkeg was no match for the Celestial--at least until he cast a suicidal spell that forged their fates into one: he captured the Celestial in the cage of his own body. Trapped together, Thunderkeg's boundless good humor fused with Raijin's crazed energy, creating the jovial Raijin Thunderkeg, a Celestial who walks the world in physical form.
    Voice:
    John Patrick Lowrie (Responses)

    • TALENT NOTES
      Raijin's attacks with the Overload buff now bounce toward a random enemy unit, without priorities, within the search radius centered around the attacked enemy unit.
    • The bounce projectiles has the following properties:
      • Does not bounce when the first attack misses.
      • The bounce projectile speed is defined as:
        • EXPR
          BounceSpeed = HeroCurrentProjectileSpeed
      • Cosmetically identical to Raijin's regular attack projectile.
    • The Overload attack bounce deals physical spell damage utilizing the bounce projectiles and its damage instance can only be affected by the following sources:
      • Before reductions-type damage manipulation sources of the target's (e.g. damage barrier sources.).
      • Bonus conditional attack damage sources that are considered by critical strike sources.
      • Damage block sources on the enemy individually upon projectile impact.
      • Bulwark, but not Resonant Pulse.
      • Has the following damage flags:
        • No-spell-lifesteal
        • No-spell-amplification
        • The flags prevent the spell damage from interacting with their respective sources.
    • In general, after reductions-type damage manipulation do not affect the bounce spell damage instance.
    • TALENT NOTES
      The bounce spell damage value is loosely defined as:
      • EXPR

    • BREAK

      • However, existing charges still grants Raijin the ability effects.
      • Does not disable attack bounce talent.
    • ABILITY DISJOINT
      Does not bounce when the primary attack target disjoints the attack projectile.
      • When disjointed by a secondary target, the attack projectile is not condsidered as a hit. The attack may immediately bounce again if it is within the search radius.

    Trivia[edit]

    • ▶️ "I'll spirit you away!", as well as him muttering "Spirit... Away..." when he dies may be a reference to the film "Spirited Away".
    • Storm Spirit's speech is very similar to Ninjor, a character from the Power Rangers franchise, whose voice acting is largely based on the character Dudley Do-Right, from the Dudley Do-Right Show.
    • Eul's Scepter of Divinity has an equivalent in Heroes of Newerth called "Stormspirit".
    • Ball lightning is an unexplained and potentially dangerous atmospheric electrical phenomenon. The term refers to reports of luminous, spherical objects that vary from pea-sized to several meters in diameter. Though usually associated with thunderstorms, the phenomenon lasts considerably longer than the split-second flash of a lightning bolt.
    • "I'll save this till it's five o'clock somewhere" is a reference to the song "It's Five O' Clock Somewhere" by Alan Jackson.
    • Warcraft 3 similarities:
      • Storm Spirit is based on and inspired by the Storm aspect, a unit summoned by the Warcraft hero, Pandaren Brewmaster.
      • Like Earth Spirit, Ember Spirit, and Brewmaster, Storm Spirit is a Pandaren, a race within the Warcraft 3 universe.
      • In Dota 2, Earth, Ember, and Storm Spirit are raceless elemental spirits instead.
      • Brewmaster's conncetion to the spirits is not expanded on in Dota 2.
