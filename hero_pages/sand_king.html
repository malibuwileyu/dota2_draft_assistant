    Bio[edit]

    I will show you fear in a handful of sand…
    ▶️  Crixalis, the Sand King
    Aliases:
    Crixalis, SK
    Lore:
    The sands of the Scintillant Waste are alive and sentient — the whole vast desert speaks to itself, thinking thoughts only such a vastness can conceive. But when it needs to find a form to communicate with those of more limited scope, it frees a fragment of itself, and fills a carapace of magic armor formed by the cunning Djinn of Qaldin. This essential identity calls itself Crixalis, meaning 'Soul of the Sand,' but others know it as Sand King. Sand King takes the form of a huge arachnid, inspired by the Scintillant Waste's small but ubiquitous denizens; and this is a true outward expression of his ferocious nature. Guardian, warrior, ambassador — Sand King is all of these things, inseparable from the endless desert that gave him life.
    Voice:
    David Scully (Responses)

    Trivia[edit]

    • Gabe Newell has claimed that Sand King is his favorite hero.[1]
    • Sand King's responses contain several cultural references:
      • ▶️ "Like the sands through an hourglass, these are the days of defeat." is an allusion to the introduction of the soap opera Days of our Lives's introduction line, "Like sands in the hourglass, these are the days of our lives.".
      • ▶️ "All we are is sand in the wind..." is an allusion to the song Dust in the Wind form the band Kansas.
      • ▶️ "You were expecting... Sandy Claws?" is likely a reference to the stop-motion film Tim Burton's The Nightmare Before Christmas.
      • ▶️ "I will show you fear in a handful of sand..." is an allusion to the poem "The Waste Land", by T. S. Eliot.
      • ▶️ "We three Kings of disorient are..." is an allusion to the Christmas Carol "We Three Kings of Orient Are", by John Henry Hopkins, Jr.
      • ▶️ "The sand of silence." is likely a reference to the song "The Sound Of Silence" by Simon and Garfunkel.
      • ▶️ "The King is dead. Long live the King." is a traditional proclamation for when a monarch dies and gets replaced by a new one.
      • ▶️ "Lion, you are king of nothing" is reference to the film "The Lion King".
    • Sand Storm's ▶️ ability sound makes use of ▶️ this and ▶️ this voice line of Sandking.
    • In very early versions of DotA, Sand King had a couple of infamous bugs.
      • One bug caused the final pulse of Epicenter to deal over 10,000 damage.
        • The value was verified by stacking a lot of strength on Pudge via Flesh Heap and check how much damage he could take.
      • Another bug involved giving Sand King the Burrowstrike cast order on an allied unit that is far away.
        • Once that ally got within cast range, Burrowstrike would send Sand King to the ally's location as the cast order was given, instead of its current location, potentially teleporting him across the map. This only worked when an ally was targeted.
        • This was commonly done to escape ganks by targeting a freshly spawned lane creep, sending Sand King towards the Barracks.
      • Dust Trail, the predecessor of Sand Storm only worked when Sand King was on team Dire (Scourge).
        • If skilled on team Radiant (Sentinel), the ability did nothing at all.
    • Warcraft 3 similarities:
      • Sand King uses the model of the Warrior Arachnathids, a medium neutral creep.
      • Burrowstrike's original iteration was inspired by the Crypt Lord's Impale.
      • Caustic Finale's original iteration was inspired by the Firelord's Incinerate.
