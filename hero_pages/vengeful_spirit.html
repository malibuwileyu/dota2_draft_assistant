    Bio[edit]

    The road of revenge is long, but what awaits me at the end makes it worthwhile.
    ‚ñ∂Ô∏è  Shendelzare, the Vengeful Spirit
    Aliases:
    Shendelzare, VS
    Lore:
    Even the most contented Skywrath is an ill-tempered creature, naturally inclined to seek revenge for the slightest insult. But Vengeful Spirit is the essence of vengeance. Once a proud and savage Skywrath scion, Shendelzare was first in succession for the Ghastly Eyrie until a sister's treachery robbed her of her birthright. Snared in an assassin's net, Shendelzare tore free only at the cost of her wings, limping away in the ultimate humiliation: On foot. With her wings broken, she knew the Skywrath would never accept her as ruler; and in the highest roost of the Eyrie, inaccessible except by winged flight, her sister was untouchable. Unwilling to live as a flightless cripple, and desiring revenge far more than earthly power, the fallen princess drove a bargain with the goddess Scree'auk: She surrendered her broken body for an imperishable form of spirit energy, driven by vengeance, capable of doing great damage in the material plane. She may spend eternity flightless, but she will have her revenge.
    Voice:
    Gin Hammond (Responses)

    Trivia[edit]

    • Previous designs of Vengeful Spirit showed her with a mask that looked similar to her Vengeance Aura ability icon.
    • In DotA, Phantom Assassin was Shendelzare's sister, but she became maniacal and murdered her, turning her into the Vengeful Spirit.
      • This is the lore explenation of why Vengeful Spirit was a Radiant (formerly Sentinel) hero, and Phantom Assassin a Dire (formerly Scourge) hero, despite being a Night Elf.
      • None of these lore aspects were kept in Dota 2, Phantom Assassin and Vengeful Spirit have no association with each other.
      • Instead, Vengeful Spirit is now a Skywrath, and is affiliated with Skywrath Mage instead.
    • Magic Missile is one of the first and most iconic offensive spells available for arcane spellcaster classes in the famed pen-and-paper RPG series, "Dungeons & Dragons".
    • Warcraft 3 similarities:
      • Vengeful Spirit uses the model of the Spirits of Vengeance, a summon of a summoned unit of the Night Elf Warden hero.
      • Magic Missile's original iteration was identical to the Mountain King's Storm Bolt.
      • Wave of Terror is based on the Pit Lord's Howl of Terror.
        • Wave of Terror's previous iteration, Terror, was identical to the Pit Lord's Howl of Terror.
      • Vengeance Aura's original iteration was identical to the Kodo Beasts' Command Aura.
        • In DotA, the aura was named Command Aura as well. It got changed to Vengeance Aura during early beta in Dota 2.
      • In DotA, Vengeful Spirit's full name is Shendelzare Silkwood. The surname was dropped to avoid copyright issues with "Blizzard Entertainment", since the Silkwood is the name of a location within the "Warcraft" universe.
      • Zeus has a 1/4 chance to spawn in with the name "820", as tribute to the professional DotA player.
