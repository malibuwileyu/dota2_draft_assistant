    Facets[edit]

    Contract Killer
    Backstab grants Riki flat bonus experience for hero kills and assists.

    Additionally grants Riki flat bonus experience from Courier kills and from killing certain ward-type units.
    Exterminator
    Tricks of the Trade is more effective on creeps, applies a greater flat bonus attack damage per current agility .

    Each non-hero unit kill while channelling reduces the ability cooldown.

    Upgrades[edit]

    Aghanim's[edit]

    Aghanim's Scepter Upgrade:
    Enables Riki to hide within a pocket dimension while moving along with the targeted ally.

    Increases cast range, max attack targets and number of instant attacks for its duration.
    Aghanim's Shard Upgrade:
    Causes enemy units to be untargetable by their allies, while reducing armor of enemies caught in the Smoke Screen.

    Bio[edit]

    There are none so stabbed as those who will not see.
    ‚ñ∂Ô∏è  Riki, the Stealth Assassin
    Aliases:
    SA, Stealth Assassin
    Lore:
    Riki was born middle child to the great dynasty of Tahlin. With an older brother groomed for the throne, and a younger brother coddled and kept, Riki, the small middle son, seemed born for the art of invisibility. It was an art he cultivated, and one which ultimately saved his life on the night that his people were betrayed and his family slaughtered. Of all the royal line, he alone escaped‚Äîsmall and agile, unassuming, using smoke as cover. He cut his way out of the royal grounds, using the advantage of surprise, quietly slitting the throats of one enemy warrior after another. Now free of his royal responsibilities, Riki uses his talents in service to a new trade: Stealth Assassin. He silences his enemies, sharpening his skills, hoping to one day take revenge on those who killed his family and robbed him of his birthright.
